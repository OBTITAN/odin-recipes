SKillet Chicken Pasta

Description

Skillet Chicken Pasta is a hearty, one-pan dish that brings together tender, seasoned chicken, al dente pasta, and a rich, flavorful sauce for a satisfying meal that's perfect for busy weeknights. This recipe combines succulent chicken breasts or thighs with a medley of garlic, onions, and tomatoes, simmered in a creamy or tomato-based sauce infused with herbs and spices. The pasta absorbs all the delicious flavors as it cooks in the same skillet, making for an easy, mess-free preparation. Whether you prefer a classic Parmesan cream sauce or a zesty marinara twist, this dish is endlessly customizable with vegetables, cheeses, or a touch of spice. Serve it hot, garnished with fresh basil and grated cheese, for a comforting, restaurant-quality meal at home

Ingredients

  • ½ (8 ounce) package spaghettini
  • 5 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 8 roma (plum) tomatoes, halved and sliced
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil over high heat. Stir in spaghettini and return to a boil. Cook pasta until it has cooked through, but is still firm to the bite, 6 to 8 minutes. Drain well and keep warm.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in tomatoes; cook until they soften and begin to break down. Stir in garlic powder, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, sugar, and ketchup. Heat sauce through and reserve.
  3. Heat remaining 3 tablespoons oil in a separate cast iron skillet over medium heat. Stir in chicken; cook until browned. Stir in garlic cloves; cook for 1 additional minute.
  4. Heat remaining 3 tablespoons oil in a separate cast iron skillet over medium heat. Stir in chicken; cook until browned. Stir in garlic cloves; cook for 1 additional minute.
  5. Toss chicken and vegetables with tomato sauce and hot pasta. Serve sprinkled with Parmesan cheese.
