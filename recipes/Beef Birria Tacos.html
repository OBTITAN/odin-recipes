Beef Birria Tacos

Description

Beef Birrias Tacos are a flavorful Mexican dish featuring slow-cooked, tender beef braised in a rich , spiced broth made with dried chies, garlic, and various seasonings. The beef is shredded and served in sofgt corn tortillas, often dipped in the savory birria broth for extra flavor before being crisp on the griddle. These tacos are typically garnished with onions and a squeeze of lime,making them delicious and comforting meal that's perfect for any occassion.

Ingredients

  • Dried chille Peppers
  • Beef
  • Seasonings
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Vinegar
  • Tortillas
  • Onion
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Boil the dried peppers for 5 minutes, then remove from heat and let the peppers soak until the water is cool. Drain, reserving some water.
  2. Season the beef and sear on all sides in oil in a Dutch oven.Season the beef and sear on all sides in oil in a Dutch oven.
  3. Grill the tomatoes until the skin is blistered and begins to peel.
  4. Blend the soaked chili peppers with the reserved chile water, tomatoes, vinegar, and seasonings.
  5. Strain the sauce and pour over the meat. Cover the Dutch oven and cook until the meat begins to fall apart, basting often. Allow the meat to rest, then shred.
  6. Warm the tortillas on the griddle. Fill each tortilla with meat, onions, and cilantro.
